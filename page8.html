About Us

Barrett Insurance is committed to integrity, customer service and education. We are here to protect your dreams. All of our agents are licensed with the State of Michigan, have a college degree and specialize in different areas of insurance. We are an independent agency which allows us to work with numerous companies and underwriters. Our advantage allows us to shop your insurance and provide the best coverage for you, your family and your business. We are here for you.

Frank Swiniarski
Licensed Agent Specializing in Commercial Insurance

About Frank

I was born in Buffalo New York, where I began my insurance career. I describe myself as a, “commercial insurance geek.”

My professional insurance career began in 1970 as a commercial insurance rater after a 4 year enlistment with the Navy. I soon moved into commercial insurance underwriting. The function of an underwriter is to analyze and price business accounts with the objective of making a profit. Once the economics of centralization and downsizing began, I moved to Columbus, Ohio where I worked for Transamerica. I managed a small department before moving to USF&G where I became a Contractor Segment Specialist. I worked with several contractors who built Jacob Field! From Columbus, I moved to Bloomington, Indiana and Great West Casualty – a company that specialized in long haul trucking. I completed my career as an in-house commercial lines specialist with a local agency.

Clint invited me to join his staff in July 2014. Although retirement is excellent and I was never bored, I decided to return to work on a limited basis. I wanted to do something tangible with the many years I invested working very hard and learning how the industry works. I decided to use what I learned to help business people with their insurance programs. The years spent with insurance companies provide me with insider knowledge as to how they work and what they do. Before computers, we hand-rated all policies. I know policy rating in detail. My experience allows me to analyze risk and estimate a fair premium. I actually read and study insurance policy forms. I compare what they say to what they should say to offer you the best protection. Regardless of how much your insurance premium is, the value of your investment is found within those 50-200 pages of policy forms and conditions which very few people review.

Briefly, I married my wife, Cindy, more than 30 years ago. We raised 5 kids and now, we like spoiling our 5 grandkids. I enjoy the outdoors. I have a constant companion in Holly, my Australian Shepard. I also take great care of three awesome horses.

Clint Barrett grew up on a crop farm in Camden, Michigan where he served as a leader with Future Farmers of America. He earned a Bachelor’s of Business degree from Michigan State University. After college, Clint worked as a manager for Citizens Insurance in Lansing. He became passionate about insurance and started his own agency in Hillsdale. Clint Barrett founded Barrett Insurance in 2001. Clint and Cyndi’s love for Michigan State University led to the colors we continue to use, today!

Clint also earned a Masters of Management degree from Aquinas College and the Certified Insurance Counselor designation after completing hours of study, testing, and rigorous training. Clint continuously improved his methods and practices to better serve our clients. He knew how insurance companies work, how policy language reads, and how to protect you with the strongest coverage plans available. 

In his spare time, Clint served on the Hillsdale Community Foundation and Hillsdale Community Health Center boards. Clint unexpectedly passed away August, 2015, at the age of 49. Barrett Insurance remains committed to Clint’s principles based on integrity, education, and service.
